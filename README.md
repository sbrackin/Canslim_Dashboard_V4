## ğŸ§  About CAN SLIM

CAN SLIM is an acronym representing:
- **C**: Current quarterly earnings
- **A**: Annual earnings growth
- **N**: New product, service, or price high
- **S**: Supply and demand
- **L**: Leader or laggard
- **I**: Institutional sponsorship
- **M**: Market direction

This dashboard scores stocks on these 7 principles.
